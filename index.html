Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Obstetrics & Gynaecology

The Department of Obstetrics and Gynaecology Basant Hospital is dedicated to women’s health and wellbeing. It provides comprehensive medical treatment for gynaecological problems as well as obstetric services.

Paediatrics

Paediatrics

Paediatrics is a branch of medicine that deals with the health and disease of children. At Basant Hospital, the department consists of a children’s ward, a paediatric intensive care unit and a special care neonatal unit.

Surgery

Surgery

The Department of Surgery is one of the oldest departments at Basant Hospital. With its dynamism many offshoots like Burns and Plastic Surgery, Neurosurgery, have grown and become full-fledged departments.

Cardiology

Cardiology

Comprehensive cares ranging from emergency angioplasty for acute heart attack in the middle of the night to educating the public about healthy life style are carried out with lot of enthusiasm.

Dermatology & Cosmetology

Dermatology & Cosmetology

At the time of inception of Basant Hospital, Dermatology started as a small section functioning under the Department of Medicine. Gradually its popularity increased and it became a flourishing unit

Psychiatry

Psychiatry

The Department of Psychiatry, Basant Hospital takes care of the different aspects of mental health and provides quality treatment to the patients.
